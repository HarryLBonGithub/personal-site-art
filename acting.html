Harry was living, working, and performing in Milwaukee Wisconsin for almost ten years before moving to Guelph Ontario. He worked on the film Baraboo which released in 2009, graduated from Marquette University with a B.A. in theater studies in 2011, and has worked with Milwaukee theater companies like Pink Banana and The Alchemist since then. Most recently he had a blast performing with the sketch comedy group Variety Hour Happy Hour, in shows like Stars War: One Magic Sithmas. When he's got time to himself, he works on self produced animated projects like the web series Space Station-19 and series pilot Most Fowl. Outside of acting, Harry enjoys playing music, dabbling in game design, and practicing web development.
